
WA2 has a goal to help us build better systems, we have some tenets to guide us:
* **small, simple, obvious**: we want to WA2 to be easy to learn and use. be nice.
* **trustworthy, with evidence**: the goal of helping build better systems requires establish a chain of evidence that can be inspected.
* **evidence chain**: allow the path from cause to result to be walked as (signed) chain of evidence.

> [!NOTE]
> These are subject to change, its early days, and we will add and evolve as we learn.
